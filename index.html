About
I am a design-focused engineer, currently a rising sophomore at Stanford University pursuing a major in Computer Science, concentrating in Human Computer Interaction, along with a side of music and product design. Here is my resume.
I'm also a bit of a musician, my instrument of choice being the keyboards/piano. I'm still exploring my sound, but I'm influenced by styles found in progressive rock, jazz, classical, and Indian music. Head over to SoundCloud and check out some of my work.
I'm deeply passionate about UI/UX design because I feel design is an essential part of any product, intergral to it's functionality, and not just an embellishment added for aesthetic purposes. It's not just about something looks, it's about how it feels and works. I believe the world can be greatly benefitted by good design.
